📱 Mobile App Features

🌟 Key Features

  • Offline Support: Works in low-connectivity rural areas
  • Real-time Sync: Data syncs when connection is available
  • QR Code Scanner: Quick animal and drug identification
  • Voice Notes: Record treatment details hands-free
  • Photo Documentation: Visual evidence of treatments
  • GPS Tracking: Location-based farm management

🛡️ Compliance Features

  • MRL Monitoring: Real-time residue level tracking
  • Withdrawal Period Calculator: Automated safety periods
  • Vet Prescription Integration: Digital prescription management
  • Audit Trail: Complete treatment history
  • Compliance Alerts: Smart notifications for violations

📊 Data Analytics

  • Treatment Patterns: Usage analytics and trends
  • Cost Analysis: Treatment cost optimization
  • Health Insights: Animal health trend analysis
  • Predictive Alerts: AI-powered health predictions
